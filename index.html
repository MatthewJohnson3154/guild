Relax. It's Time to Raid.
Our Raiding Philosophy

At <Relax> we have focused on creating an environment that promotes research and learning to improve your gameplay while maintaining a casual schedule. While we may only be raiding in game 6-9 hours a week, we want our members to take any amount of time they can outside of the game to do things such as research their class or read up on boss strats. Doing so will likely boost their contribution every night, and as we progress, we can indicate who puts in that extra effort, and mold our Mythic progression around that team.

Our current plan is to raid at the Normal and Heroic level until we are able to take a crack at Mythic.
